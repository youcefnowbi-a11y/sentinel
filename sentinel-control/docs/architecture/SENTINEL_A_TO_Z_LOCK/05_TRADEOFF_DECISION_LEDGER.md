# Tradeoff Decision Ledger

## Power Vs Safety

Decision: Sentinel wants real action, but every action passes authority,
evidence, trace, receipts, and FinalGate.

## Speed Vs Proof

Decision: safe local actions can be fast. External, irreversible, sensitive,
costly, or high-impact actions require dry-run, approval, and receipts.

## Memory Vs Authority

Decision: memory improves continuity but never grants tools, spend,
credentials, accounts, or permissions.

## Multi-Agent Scale Vs Cost

Decision: more agents only when entropy, parallelizability, expected
information gain, and budget justify them.

## Browser Power Vs Compliance

Decision: Cloak-like browser powers are not discarded. They are classified and
governed. The Brain may use lower or stronger levels only when authority,
legality, domain policy, evidence, expected value, receipts, and FinalGate
support use.

## Financial Expertise Vs Financial Action

Decision: finance skills can analyze, model, audit, draft, and prepare
opportunities. They do not provide final professional advice, execute trades,
post ledgers, approve onboarding, or guarantee profit without future special
authority and human review gates.

## Vendor Reuse Vs Originality

Decision: take mechanisms and lessons; never import vendor runtime/code/bridges
as product shortcuts.
