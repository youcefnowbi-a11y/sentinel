# Preservation Constraints

## Architecture Constraints

```text
Do not copy vendor code.
Do not bridge vendor runtime.
Do not run vendor sidecars/plugins/skills as part of Sentinel.
Do not grant authority from memory, skills, prompts, docs, or browser content.
Do not add external powers without organ promotion.
Do not treat spend/trading/account creation as normal actions.
Do not let dynamic operating envelopes cross root authority.
Do not lose P5L Brain L4 certification.
Do not overwrite unrelated dirty-tree work.
```

## Cloak Power Constraint

Cloak-like powers are harvested, not ignored. Misuse objectives are blocked, not
the existence of the capability.

## Finance Constraint

Finance workflows produce analyst work product for review. They do not execute
transactions, bind risk, approve onboarding, or guarantee profit.
